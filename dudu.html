"Ddu-Du Ddu-Du" ( Korean: 뚜두뚜두, ddu-du ddu-du) is a song by South Korean girl group BLACKPINK. It is the first and title track in the group's first mini-album Square Up. The single and its music video were released on June 15, 2018.
