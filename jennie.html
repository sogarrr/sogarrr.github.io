Jennie Kim

Jennie Kim or familiarly called Jennie is one of the members who occupies a position as the main rapper, lead vocalist, and center in the group. He was born in Anyang, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea on January 16, 1996. Although born in Korea, he grew up and attended school in New Zealand. Previously, he attended the Netherlands and moved to New Zealand and attended ACG Parnell College.
